Fix Grammar

Fix Grammar calls Gemini through Firebase Functions first. If Gemini is unavailable, FLOQR only applies your saved personal correction pairs and preserves your Word List.

My Word List protects names, slang, handles, and preferred spellings. My Personal Corrections handles repeated typos like watz -> what's. FLOQR does not run a generalized local typo dictionary.
